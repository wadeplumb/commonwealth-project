Beacon Academy Commonwealth

Beacon Academy seeks to be a long-lasting community dedicated to nurturing the genius, passions, and potential in each child and parent through excellence in education, leadership, and individualized mentoring.

As a Commonwealth based on the principles of A Thomas Jefferson Education, we seek to support families as they build on their Core, Love of Learning, and Scholar phases in the home. Our primary focus is on enhancing the Scholar phase through leadership, classical literature, and studying the history of Western Civilization

Our Vision statement:

Beacon Academy is a Commonwealth of families striving to be a strong Christian community
united in supporting, serving, and inspiring one another as we seek to pursue meaningful
education for our children and ourselves and fulfill our God-given missions
